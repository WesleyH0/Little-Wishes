一、我们如何理解家庭隐私

家有小愿是一个让孩子的愿望被看见的家庭成长空间。应用中可能会记录孩子的愿望、生活记录、奖励、零花钱、家庭账户、头像、设备连接状态、统计信息和 iCloud 备份状态等内容。这些内容属于家庭内部的私密记录。

我们非常重视家庭与孩子的数据安全。家有小愿的核心设计是：家庭数据主要保存在你的设备本地，设备之间通过本地连接进行同步。我们不会主动收集、查看、出售、出租或用于广告分析你的家庭记录内容。

请注意：如果你开启 iCloud 备份，相关备份数据会保存到你自己的 Apple iCloud 账户中，用于备份和恢复。我们无法读取你的 iCloud 账户内容。

1. How We Think About Family Privacy

Little Wishes is a family growth space where children’s wishes can be seen and remembered. The app may contain children’s wishes, life records, rewards, allowance records, family accounts, avatars, device connection status, statistics, and iCloud backup status. These are private family records.

We take family and children’s privacy seriously. The app is designed so that family data is mainly stored locally on your device and shared between family devices through local connection. We do not actively collect, view, sell, rent, or use your family records for advertising analytics.

If you enable iCloud Backup, backup data will be stored in your own Apple iCloud account for backup and restore. We cannot access the content of your iCloud account.

二、我们可能保存的数据类型

为了让应用正常运行，家有小愿可能会在你的设备本地保存以下数据：

  • 家庭账户信息：孩子昵称、头像、账户模式、账户设置。
  • 愿望记录：孩子写下的愿望、愿望说明、发布时间、回应内容、实现状态、实现时间。
  • 生活记录：家长发布的生活事项、完成状态、奖励金额、开始时间、完成时间、确认时间。
  • 零花钱与账本：收入、支出、余额、分类、备注、日期和统计信息。
  • 设备连接信息：用于家长设备与孩子设备进行本地连接、配对和同步。
  • 应用设置：主题、语言、货币、通知权限状态、订阅状态、本地试用状态、iCloud 备份开关。
  • 购买状态：订阅或一次性买断的状态。实际购买与支付由 Apple App Store 处理。
  • 备份数据：如果开启 iCloud 备份，会保存当前备份和每日备份，用于恢复。

2. Data That May Be Stored

To make the app work properly, Little Wishes may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Family account information: child nickname, avatar, account mode, and account settings.
  • Wish records: wishes written by children, details, creation time, family responses, fulfillment status, and fulfillment time.
  • Life records: life activities created by parents, completion status, reward amount, start time, completion time, and confirmation time.
  • Allowance and ledger records: income, expenses, balance, categories, notes, dates, and statistics.
  • Device connection information: used for local pairing and syncing between parent and child devices.
  • App settings: theme, language, currency, notification permission status, subscription status, trial status, and iCloud backup setting.
  • Purchase status: subscription or lifetime unlock status. Actual payment is handled by Apple App Store.
  • Backup data: if iCloud Backup is enabled, current and daily backups may be stored for restore purposes.

三、数据存储方式

本地保存

家有小愿的数据主要保存在你的 iPhone 或 iPad 本地。我们没有独立服务器来保存你的家庭记录，也不会把孩子的愿望、生活记录、奖励和零花钱内容上传到我们自己的服务器。

本地设备同步

家长设备和孩子设备可以通过本地网络、附近设备连接等方式进行配对和数据同步。此类同步用于家庭设备之间传递记录，不用于公开发布或第三方广告用途。

iCloud 备份

如果你购买高级版并开启 iCloud 备份，应用会将备份数据保存到你自己的 Apple iCloud 账户中。iCloud 备份可帮助你在误删数据、更换设备或重新安装应用后尝试恢复记录。

iCloud 备份恢复的是应用数据，不代表新设备已经与原孩子设备完成连接。如果新的家长设备从 iCloud 恢复了数据，但之前没有和孩子设备配对过，仍可能需要重新进行家庭设备绑定。

3. How Data Is Stored

Local Storage

Little Wishes mainly stores data locally on your iPhone or iPad. We do not operate a separate server to store your family records, and we do not upload children’s wishes, life records, rewards, or allowance records to our own server.

Local Device Sync

Parent and child devices may be paired and synced through local network or nearby device connection. This sync is used to transfer records between family devices, not for public sharing or third-party advertising.

iCloud Backup

If you purchase the premium version and enable iCloud Backup, backup data may be stored in your own Apple iCloud account. iCloud Backup can help you restore records after accidental deletion, device replacement, or app reinstallation.

iCloud Backup restores app data, but it does not automatically mean that a new device has already paired with the original child device. If a new parent device restores data from iCloud but has never paired with the child device, family device pairing may need to be set up again.

四、我们不会做什么

  • 我们不会出售你的家庭数据。
  • 我们不会将孩子的愿望、生活记录或零花钱记录用于广告画像。
  • 我们不会主动查看你的家庭记录内容。
  • 我们不会要求你创建我们的服务器账户才能使用核心本地功能。
  • 我们不会把家庭记录公开发布到互联网。

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your family data.
  • We do not use children’s wishes, life records, or allowance records for advertising profiles.
  • We do not actively view your family record content.
  • We do not require you to create our own server account to use core local features.
  • We do not publish family records to the internet.

五、Apple、App Store 与第三方服务

家有小愿通过 Apple App Store 提供订阅和一次性买断。支付、退款、订阅管理、家庭共享和购买凭证由 Apple 处理。我们无法获取你的完整银行卡、Apple ID 密码或支付账户敏感信息。

如果使用 iCloud 备份，数据会由 Apple iCloud 服务保存到你的 Apple 账户中。Apple 对相关服务有自己的隐私政策和条款。

5. Apple, App Store, and Third-Party Services

Little Wishes offers subscriptions and lifetime unlock through Apple App Store. Payment, refunds, subscription management, Family Sharing, and purchase receipts are handled by Apple. We do not receive your full bank card number, Apple ID password, or sensitive payment account information.

If iCloud Backup is used, data is stored by Apple iCloud under your Apple account. Apple’s own privacy policy and terms apply to those services.

六、儿童隐私说明

家有小愿面向家庭共同使用，尤其适合有一定表达和理解能力的孩子与家长一起使用。孩子可以写下愿望、查看生活记录、查看零花钱变化，家长可以创建账户、发布生活记录、回应愿望和管理家庭记录。

我们建议家长在孩子使用前，先向孩子说明应用的用途，并一起约定哪些内容适合记录。请不要在应用中记录身份证号、住址、学校详细地址、银行卡号、医疗隐私等高度敏感信息。

6. Children’s Privacy

Little Wishes is designed for family use, especially for children who can express and understand basic family records together with their parents. Children can write wishes, view life records, and check allowance changes. Parents can create accounts, publish life records, respond to wishes, and manage family records.

We recommend that parents explain the purpose of the app to children before use and agree together on what is appropriate to record. Please do not record highly sensitive information such as ID numbers, home addresses, detailed school addresses, bank card numbers, or medical privacy information in the app.

七、通知权限

在孩子模式下，应用可能请求系统通知权限，用于在有新的生活记录、收入或家庭提醒时通知孩子。你可以随时在 iOS 系统设置中关闭通知权限。

通知内容只用于提醒家庭内部事件，不用于广告推送。

7. Notifications

In child mode, the app may request notification permission to notify the child about new life records, income, or family reminders. You can turn off notifications at any time in iOS Settings.

Notifications are used only for family-related reminders, not for advertising.

八、数据删除与恢复

你可以在应用内删除账户、生活记录、愿望或账本记录。删除后，这些数据可能会从本机记录中移除，并可能通过家庭设备同步影响已连接设备上的对应记录。

如果你删除 App，设备本地保存的数据通常也会被删除。如果你此前开启了 iCloud 备份，并且使用同一个 Apple ID 重新安装应用，应用可能可以从 iCloud 中恢复当前备份或最近备份。但恢复能力取决于 iCloud 是否开启、备份是否成功、Apple ID 是否一致、iCloud 空间与系统状态是否正常。

为了避免重要家庭记录丢失，建议在删除 App、换机或清理数据前，确认 iCloud 备份已经开启并完成。

8. Data Deletion and Restore

You may delete accounts, life records, wishes, or ledger records in the app. After deletion, the data may be removed from the local device and may also affect corresponding records on paired family devices through sync.

If you delete the app, locally stored data is usually deleted as well. If iCloud Backup was enabled before deletion and you reinstall the app with the same Apple ID, the app may be able to restore the current or recent backup from iCloud. Restore availability depends on whether iCloud was enabled, whether backup succeeded, whether the Apple ID is the same, and whether iCloud storage and system status are normal.

To avoid losing important family records, please make sure iCloud Backup is enabled and completed before deleting the app, changing devices, or clearing data.

九、数据安全

我们会尽力采用符合平台能力的方式保护应用数据，例如使用 iOS 本地存储、系统权限、Apple StoreKit 购买验证和 iCloud 账户能力。但任何本地设备或云端备份都无法保证绝对安全。

请妥善保管你的设备、锁屏密码、Apple ID 和 iCloud 账户。不要把设备长期交给不信任的人使用。

9. Data Security

We try to protect app data using platform-supported methods, such as iOS local storage, system permissions, Apple StoreKit purchase verification, and iCloud account capabilities. However, no local device or cloud backup can be guaranteed to be absolutely secure.

Please protect your device, passcode, Apple ID, and iCloud account. Do not leave your device with untrusted people for a long time.
