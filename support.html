Welcome to Little Wishes

Little Wishes is a family growth space where children’s wishes can be seen and remembered. Children can write wishes, and parents can record life moments, respond to expectations, manage allowance, and keep warm memories of small efforts.

If you encounter any issue, please check the guide below first. If the issue remains, contact us at 764725516@qq.com. Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, screenshots, and steps to reproduce the issue so we can help faster.

一、适合哪些设备和家庭

  • 支持 iPhone 和 iPad。
  • 适合家长与 8 岁以上孩子共同使用。
  • 推荐家长设备选择“家长模式”，孩子设备选择“孩子模式”。
  • 如果家庭中有多台设备，建议先在家长设备创建孩子账户，再让孩子设备进行连接。

1. Supported Devices and Families

  • Supports iPhone and iPad.
  • Designed for parents and children aged 8 and above.
  • We recommend using “Parent Mode” on the parent device and “Child Mode” on the child device.
  • If your family uses multiple devices, create the child account on the parent device first, then connect the child device.

二、第一次使用建议步骤

  1. 在一台 iPhone 或 iPad 上打开家有小愿，选择家长模式。
  2. 在家长模式中创建孩子账户。
  3. 在另一台孩子使用的 iPhone 或 iPad 上打开家有小愿，选择孩子模式。
  4. 让两台设备尽量靠近，并连接到同一个 Wi-Fi 网络，会更稳定。
  5. 在家长设备中搜索并连接孩子设备。
  6. 连接完成后，就可以发布生活记录、记录零花钱、回应愿望和同步家庭内容。
如果暂时只有一台设备，也可以先在家长模式中进行记录。后续需要孩子设备一起使用时，再进行连接。

2. Recommended First-Time Setup

  1. Open Little Wishes on an iPhone or iPad and choose Parent Mode.
  2. Create a child account in Parent Mode.
  3. Open Little Wishes on the child’s iPhone or iPad and choose Child Mode.
  4. Keep both devices nearby and connect them to the same Wi-Fi network for better stability.
  5. Search for and connect the child device from the parent device.
  6. After connection, you can publish life records, manage allowance, respond to wishes, and sync family content.
If you only have one device for now, you can start recording in Parent Mode and connect a child device later.

三、核心功能说明

愿望

孩子可以写下自己的小愿望。家长可以看到、回应并帮助实现。愿望实现后会被保存下来，成为可以回看的幸福记录。

生活

家长可以发布生活记录，比如阅读、运动、整理、家务或学习计划。孩子开始后可以完成，家长确认后记录结果。

零花钱

支持记录收入、支出、奖励和余额变化，帮助孩子慢慢理解积累、选择和责任感。

详细统计

可以查看收入支出、生活记录、愿望实现等情况，并将统计页面分享给家人。

家庭连接

家长设备和孩子设备可通过本地连接进行同步。家庭内容主要保存在设备本地。

iCloud 备份

高级版可使用 iCloud 备份，帮助在误删、换机或重新安装后尝试恢复数据。

3. Main Features

Wishes

Children can write down their wishes. Parents can see, respond, and help fulfill them. Fulfilled wishes are saved as warm memories.

Life

Parents can create life records such as reading, exercise, tidying up, chores, or learning plans. Children can start and complete them, and parents can confirm the result.

Allowance

Record income, expenses, rewards, and balance changes to help children understand saving, choices, and responsibility.

Statistics

View income, expenses, life records, and fulfilled wishes. You can share the statistics page with family members.

Family Connection

Parent and child devices can sync through local connection. Family data is mainly stored locally on your devices.

iCloud Backup

Premium users can use iCloud Backup to help restore data after accidental deletion, device replacement, or app reinstallation.

四、连接或同步失败怎么办

  • 确认两台设备都已打开家有小愿，并处于同一局域网或彼此靠近。
  • 确认家长设备选择家长模式，孩子设备选择孩子模式。
  • 确认孩子设备还没有被其他家庭账户绑定；已绑定的孩子设备通常不会重复广播。
  • 尝试让两台设备连接到同一个 Wi-Fi 网络，通常会比仅靠附近连接更稳定。
  • 检查 iOS 设置中是否允许本地网络权限、蓝牙权限或通知权限。
  • 关闭并重新打开应用，再重新进入附近连接页面。
  • 如果仍无法连接，可以重启设备后再试。
本地同步依赖 iOS 系统允许的连接环境。当 App 完全关闭、系统挂起或网络环境不稳定时，实时同步和提醒可能会延迟。

4. What to Do If Connection or Sync Fails

  • Make sure both devices have Little Wishes open and are on the same local network or nearby.
  • Make sure the parent device is in Parent Mode and the child device is in Child Mode.
  • Make sure the child device is not already paired with another family account. A paired child device usually will not broadcast again.
  • Try connecting both devices to the same Wi-Fi network, which is usually more stable than nearby connection alone.
  • Check iOS Settings and allow Local Network, Bluetooth, or Notification permissions if requested.
  • Close and reopen the app, then enter the nearby connection page again.
  • If the issue remains, restart both devices and try again.
Local sync depends on the connection conditions allowed by iOS. When the app is fully closed, suspended by the system, or the network is unstable, real-time sync and reminders may be delayed.

五、通知没有弹出怎么办

  • 在 iOS 设置中确认家有小愿的通知权限已经开启。
  • 确认当前设备是孩子模式，且已经与家长设备连接过。
  • 如果应用正在前台打开，系统通知可能不会弹出，因为应用内已经能看到更新。
  • 如果 App 被系统完全挂起或关闭，本地连接消息可能无法立即到达，通知也可能无法立即触发。

5. What to Do If Notifications Do Not Appear

  • Make sure notification permission for Little Wishes is enabled in iOS Settings.
  • Make sure the device is in Child Mode and has been connected to the parent device.
  • If the app is open in the foreground, system notifications may not appear because updates are already visible in the app.
  • If the app is fully suspended or closed by the system, local connection messages may not arrive immediately, so notifications may also be delayed.

七、iCloud 备份和恢复说明

  • iCloud 备份需要使用同一个 Apple ID。
  • 请确认 iCloud 可用、空间足够，并且系统允许应用使用 iCloud。
  • 备份用于保存应用数据，例如账户、愿望、生活记录、零花钱和设置。
  • 删除 App 后，本机数据通常会被删除；如果之前成功开启 iCloud 备份，重新安装后可能可以恢复。
  • iCloud 恢复的是数据，不会自动完成新设备和孩子设备之间的连接。新设备可能需要重新绑定孩子设备。
  • 如果误删数据，可以尝试使用“手动还原到昨天备份”。
iCloud 备份并不是服务器实时同步。它主要用于备份与恢复，实际效果受 Apple ID、iCloud 状态、系统权限、网络和备份成功时间影响。

7. iCloud Backup and Restore

  • iCloud Backup requires the same Apple ID.
  • Make sure iCloud is available, storage is enough, and the system allows the app to use iCloud.
  • Backup is used to save app data such as accounts, wishes, life records, allowance records, and settings.
  • After deleting the app, local data is usually removed. If iCloud Backup was enabled successfully before, the data may be restored after reinstalling.
  • iCloud restore restores app data, but does not automatically pair a new device with the child device. You may need to pair the child device again.
  • If data is accidentally deleted, try “Restore Yesterday’s Backup”.
iCloud Backup is not a real-time server sync service. It is mainly for backup and restore, and its availability depends on Apple ID, iCloud status, system permissions, network, and backup time.

八、常见问题

1. 免费版可以使用什么？

免费试用结束后，仍可进行基础记录。多账户、家庭连接、愿望、生活记录、自动收入、iCloud 备份等高级功能可能需要订阅或买断后使用，具体以应用内显示为准。

2. 孩子可以删除愿望吗？

孩子模式下可以删除自己误添加的愿望。家长模式下可以折叠愿望，减少列表干扰，但折叠不代表愿望已完成，也不会改变孩子看到的愿望状态。

3. 为什么生活记录完成按钮会倒计时？

孩子点击开始后，需要等待短暂倒计时才可以完成，这是为了减少误触和刚开始就立即完成的情况。

4. 删除 App 后数据还在吗？

删除 App 通常会删除本机数据。如果之前开启并成功完成 iCloud 备份，重新安装后可能可以恢复；如果没有备份，删除后可能无法找回。

5. 可以导出账本吗？

账本页面支持导出 CSV 表格，方便保存或整理家庭记录。

8. Frequently Asked Questions

1. What can I use after the free trial?

After the free trial ends, basic records may still be available. Advanced features such as multiple accounts, family connection, wishes, life records, automatic income, and iCloud Backup may require subscription or lifetime unlock. Please refer to the in-app display.

2. Can children delete wishes?

In Child Mode, children can delete wishes they added by mistake. In Parent Mode, parents can fold wishes to reduce list clutter, but folding does not mean completion and does not change the wish status shown to the child.

3. Why is there a countdown before completing a life record?

After a child taps Start, a short countdown is required before completion. This helps reduce accidental taps or immediate completion right after starting.

4. Will data remain after deleting the app?

Deleting the app usually removes local data. If iCloud Backup was enabled and completed before deletion, data may be restored after reinstalling. Without backup, deleted data may not be recoverable.

5. Can I export ledger records?

The ledger page supports CSV export, making it easier to save or organize family records.
